Essential Booking Apps & Platforms

GetYourGuide Tours & day trips across all 5 countries. Strong in Vietnam & Thailand. Good for group activities.
Klook Best for SE Asia. Often cheaper than GYG. Excellent for Thailand, Philippines, Indonesia. Also has transport.
Viator Similar to GetYourGuide. Sometimes has unique local operators and experiences.
12Go.asia THE app for trains, buses, ferries across SE Asia. Essential for Vietnam trains, Thai trains, Lao railway, all ferries.
Baolau.com Vietnam trains specifically. Sometimes has tickets that 12Go doesn't have available.
Hostelworld Best for backpacker hostels across all 5 countries. Filter by social atmosphere & facilities.
Booking.com Largest range overall. Excellent for guesthouses & mid-range hotels. Good reviews & cancellation policies.
Agoda Often cheapest for SE Asia hotels & hostels. Always compare against Booking.com for best price.
Grab SE Asia's Uber equivalent. Use for taxis & rides in Vietnam, Thailand, Philippines, Indonesia.
Lomprayah Book Thai island ferries direct (Koh Phangan, Koh Tao, Chumphon). Often cheaper than booking through hostels.
Cebu Pacific / AirAsia Apps Book budget flights direct for cheapest fares. Avoid paying through third-party sites.
Google Maps Download offline maps for each country before arriving. Essential for navigation without data.
